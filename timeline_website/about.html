About

A better perspective on time

Timeline is a time visualization and management application. As a visualizer, Timeline breaks the trend of displaying time as a calendar or grid. Using the familiar linear concept of a timeline, our application intends to present a user's schedule of events in a more appealing format. As a manager, Timeline allows users to share their timeline-based schedules with one another to automatically see feedback about shared events, and our favorite - shared free time.


By sharing and combining timelines in the Timeline app, users can effortlessly determine when members of a friend group, or project team are available to hang out, or get some work done.
